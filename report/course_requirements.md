For your final project report submission, you will use the SIEDS Conference IEEE paper format. The link below under the "Paper Formatting" tab has template options for the final report (Word, Google Doc, Overleaf, etc.). You should submit ALL supporting files for your project (e.g. data collection sheets, developed code, simulation files, etc.). The files you submit should be clearly labelled with what they are.

The final project report will be worth 80% of the final project grade (the remaining 20% is split equally between your submitted slide deck and presentation). We will grade your report based on the quality and effectiveness of your written document and supporting files, and the overall logical approach your team took for the problem under study. An important component of this project is validation of your model(s); you should clearly outline in your paper submission how you validated your model(s) and the result of it.
